ℹ️ How GPS Restriction Works
🏢 Office
Punch is accepted only when the employee is within the office radius. GPS coordinates are checked against the office location set above. Rejected punches show distance and direction.
🏠 WFH
Punch is accepted only from within the employee's registered home coordinates. Set home lat/lng per employee. Radius is configurable (recommended 300–500m to account for GPS drift).
🚗 Liaison
No location restriction. Employee can punch from anywhere. GPS coordinates are still captured and logged with every punch for audit purposes. Supervisors can review punch locations.
